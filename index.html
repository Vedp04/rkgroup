About Company

RK Group is a leading manufacturer located in Vadodara, Gujarat, specializing in high-quality engineering solutions. We offer a diverse range of products, including machinery parts and industrial bushes, such as headed shafts and shaft bushes, all designed to meet the rigorous standards of our clients. Our commitment to innovation and excellence drives us to continually enhance our manufacturing processes.

As an partnership firm, we prioritize building strong, long-term relationships with our clients by delivering reliable and cost-effective solutions tailored to their needs.
